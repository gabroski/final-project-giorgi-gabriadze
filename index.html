Luka Mshvildadze

Senior Mathematics Lecturer

Luka Mshvildadze is a distinguished mathematics educator with over 15 years of experience in teaching advanced mathematics concepts. He specializes in calculus, linear algebra, and mathematical analysis, making complex mathematical theories accessible to students of all levels.

With a Ph.D. in Mathematics from Tbilisi State University and extensive research experience, Luka has developed innovative teaching methodologies that combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications. His passion for mathematics and dedication to student success has earned him recognition as one of the leading mathematics educators in the region.

Luka's teaching approach focuses on building strong foundational skills while encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving abilities. He believes that every student has the potential to excel in mathematics with the right guidance and support.
